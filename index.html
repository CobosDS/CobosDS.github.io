Get to know me!

Hello! I'm a dedicated Data Scientist adept in unraveling complex data challenges. My journey has seen me engaging in various projects aimed at enhancing operations for the HORECA sector. Leveraging a blend of technical expertise and industry knowledge, I've specialized in creating comprehensive censuses, integrating data pipelines with ETL processes using Microsoft Azure (including Databricks, Data Lake, and Data Factory), and developing expert customer matching models.

My knack for segmentation and potential customer valuation models has empowered businesses to unlock actionable insights and drive strategic decisions. If you're intrigued by data's potential or seeking expertise in data-driven transformation, feel free to get in touch.
