Exploring Global Happiness

A comprehensive analysis of happiness factors across the globe, using statistical methods in R to explore socio-economic indicators and their impact on happiness.

View Notebook

Project Overview

This project delves into the happiness scores of various countries, examining how different socio-economic factors such as GDP, health, freedom, and social support influence overall happiness. The analysis was conducted using R and involved linear and logistic regression models to uncover the intricate relationships between these variables

The project highlights the power of data science in understanding complex social phenomena, offering valuable insights into what contributes to happiness across diverse nations.
